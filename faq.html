Frequently Asked Questions

Mudika Big Game 2021

1. Are we allowed to use Google?

Yes! In fact, Google is essential for solving several of the puzzles.


2. What should we do when we finish solving all the puzzles?

Figure out the 3Ws: who took Valerie, where is she, and why. Then use the call for help Zoom function. Clarissa will come to your room to take final answers.


3. How does my group call for help/get a hint?

Click on the Call For Help button near the bottom of the Zoom call. Wait for Clarissa to come into your room. Work on other puzzles as you wait.


4. How much time do we have?

50 minutes


5. How are the winners decided?

By time. We will keep track of which groups call Clarissa to give their final answers first. Points will also be deducted based on number of hints used.
