Frequently Asked Questions

Mudika Big Game 2021

1. Are we allowed to use Google?

Yes! In fact, Google is essential for solving several of the puzzles.


2. What should we do when we finish solving all the puzzles?

Figure out the 3Ws: who took Valerie, where is she, and why. Then return to the main Zoom room and give Clarissa your answers.


3. How does my group call for help/get a hint?

Click on the Call For Help button at the bottom right of the Zoom call. Wait for Clarissa to come into your room. Work on other puzzles as you wait.

4. How much time do we have?

50 minutes
