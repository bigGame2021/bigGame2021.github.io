Welcome to the annual Mudika Big Game...online edition!

Your group will split into two equal groups A and B but your entire group will still be working together.
Here are a few house rules before you start:

  • No screen sharing in any form is allowed. This includes taking pictures of your screen and holding them up to your camera for your teammates to see, sending screen shots, etc.
  • Do not have both windows for Group A and Group B open. If you are in Group A, you should only have A's window open. If you are in Group B, you should only have B's window open.

When your entire team is ready, everybody may click their associated links below to start. Work together with your teammates to solve the puzzles and have fun!
